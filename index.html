About Me

Welcome to my website! My name is Lawrence Abu-Hammour and I'm currently a junior at Maritime College studying Mechanical Engineering. I'm a Dartmouth-educated and hard-working engineer/historian/problem-solver/creative and I have always been fascinated with the world of design and engineering ever since I was a kid. I've always had a love for History because of how fun it can be to learn the story of how people lived and continue to live today. I'm from the Boogey-Down Bronx in the great New York City (see picture above and see map below). It is my home and it will always be my home. One day, I plan to use the skills that I've learned throughout my experiences in Engineering, Design, and History to give back to the community that raised me and help to build up the next generation of Bronxites ready to take on the world. I like to dabble in a lot of different subject matters, so to make things easier, I pose the question to you, what are you interested in?
